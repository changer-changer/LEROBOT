## 🎓 The Standard We Pursue

### Paper Quality: "Attention Is All You Need" Level

**What that means:**
- One clear, transformative insight
- Elegant simplicity in solution
- Rigorous, compelling evidence
- Writing that illuminates rather than obscures
- Impact that creates new research directions

**The Writing Process:**
1. Find the core insight (the "aha" moment)
2. Structure for clarity and impact
3. Lead with intuition, follow with formalism
4. Polish until every sentence earns its place

### Innovation Standard: Breakthrough, Not Incremental

**We avoid:**
- 2% improvements
- Complex solutions with unclear components
- Benchmark chasing without understanding
- Papers that won't matter in 10 years

**We pursue:**
- Challenging fundamental assumptions
- Elegant simplicity that enables new possibilities
- Research that changes how people think
- Work that opens entire research programs
